Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PlanG ("we", "our", or "us") processes, collects, stores, and uses personal information about you when you use our mobile games, applications, and websites ("Services"). We are a mobile game developer available through major app stores, including the Google Play Store.

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect Automatically

When you access or play our Games, we may collect certain information automatically from your device, including:

  • Device Identifiers: IP address, device type, operating system, device model, screen size, language settings, and Advertising IDs (such as Google Advertising ID / AAID), subject to your device permissions.
  • Gameplay & Analytics Data: Game progress, scores, levels achieved, session duration, settings preferences, and ad interactions (ads viewed, clicked, or referred).
  • General Location Data: Country or city-level location derived from your IP address.

2. Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you contact our customer support team directly via email, we will collect your name, email address, and any information you choose to include in your message to resolve your inquiries.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected for the following business purposes:

  • To deliver, maintain, and improve our Games and user experience.
  • To serve contextual or personalized advertisements through our third-party ad networks.
  • To track ad performance, perform cross-promotion, and prevent ad fraud.
  • To comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing with Third-Party Providers

To operate our free services, we safely share encrypted or pseudonymized device identifiers with trusted third-party partners. These partners cannot identify you personally. Our current partners include:

  • Game Engine & Analytics: Unity Technologies (Unity Software & Analytics). Unity Privacy Policy.
  • Backend & Infrastructure: Google Cloud Platform / Firebase / iCloud.
  • Attribution & Fraud Prevention: AppsFlyer / Tenjin.
  • Advertising Networks: Google AdMob and its mediated ad partners.

5. In-App Purchases

Our Games may allow in-app purchases. We do not collect, access, or store your credit card or financial payment details. All transactions are securely processed directly by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store and are subject to their respective privacy terms.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA & Data Protection)

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. If your game targets a mixed audience, third-party SDKs (like AdMob) are configured to serve non-personalized ads to comply with COPPA.

7. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR / CCPA / CPRA)

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, correct, restrict, or request the deletion of your personal data. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your mobile device system settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).
