Welcome to the official presentation of Plan G Game Studio. We are a creative studio dedicated to video game development with an innovative and dynamic approach.

🚀 Our Mission

At Plan G, our mission is to design immersive worlds and game mechanics that challenge the players' imagination. We specialize in creating experiences where gameplay and narrative meet to tell unique stories.

💎 The "Plan G" (Our Identity)

Our brand stands out for the G, which symbolizes the heart of what we do: Games. In our DNA, we carry the spirit of classic controllers, represented in our visual identity, merging minimalist design with the energy of modern gaming.

🛠️ What We Do

  • Indie Development: Creating original titles with fresh mechanics.
  • Level Design: Building detailed and immersive environments.
  • Art & Animation: Polished visual aesthetics (Minimalist Motion Graphics).
  • Interactive Narrative: Stories where the player is in control.
